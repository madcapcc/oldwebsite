About MadCap

At MadCap, we're more than just traditional dotwatching. We offer cutting-edge GPS trackers for ultra cycling races and an intuitive tracking platform that takes your experience to the next level.

Our Services

  • GPS Trackers: We provide high-quality GPS trackers designed to withstand the most challenging ultra cycling races. These trackers offer real-time location updates, ensuring your event stays on track.
  • Tracking Platform: Our user-friendly tracking platform allows friends, family, and race enthusiasts to dotwatch the race live. Get real-time updates and cheer on your favorite racers, no matter where you are.
  • Rent and Delivery: Unlike other companies, we make it hassle-free. We rent the trackers and deliver them directly to event organizers. We also provide the necessary software to get you started, and it's all designed with a mobile-first approach.
